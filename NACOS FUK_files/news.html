Registration Procedures

This is to inform all members that the registration for membership is as follows:

1. Go to the top right corner of the website and click on the toggle icon (=)

2. Click on Registration

3. Click on NACOS Membership

4. Fill in your details as required

5. Wait for review and approval.

Upon successful verification, a notification will be sent to your registered email (check your spam or promotion folder if not found in the Primary folder) within a limited time after submitting your registration.

Or Call your Class Representative to approve your account.

NACOS FUK Smart Association

Hi everyone,

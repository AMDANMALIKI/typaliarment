Who we are?

We are committed to running an all-inclusive parliament that will work with the young people domiciled in Nigeria and the Diaspora for National re-orientation and Nigeri's positive image projection worldwide.
We Function for the Youths, Work to change things for the youths and work with them to give birth to the bright future for the Nation.

Aims and Objectives

*To Debate on issues of Local, State and National Importance.
*To get Taraba Youth Invloved in Governance and Decision Making.
*To mentor Taraba Youth on Legislative Processes.
*To Depend and foster unity amongst Taraba Yoth.
*To project the image of Taraba Youth by way of seminars, symposia, lectures, workshops and other activities aimed at achieving this goal.
*To discourage tribal, religious and gender sentiment for the unity of our country.
To encourage youth on patriotism and embrace peace at all level.
To enlightened the youth on their constitutional rights and responsibilities as citizens of Nigeria.

Our Vision

YOUTH INCLUSIVENESS IN GOVERNANCE AND PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION.
YOUTH DEVELOPMENT AND EMPOWERMENT FUND WHICH WILL DRIVE THE NIGERIAN ECONOMIC, AMONG NIGERIAN YOUTHS.
ESTABLISHMENT OF YOUTH PARLIAMENT IN LGA.
